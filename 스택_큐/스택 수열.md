## 스택 수열

```python
n=int(input())
max=0
stack=[]
answer=[]
for i in range(n):
  m=int(input())
  if m in stack :
    for i in range(len(stack),0,-1):
      
      answer.append('-')
  for i in range(max,m+1):
    stack.append(i+1)
    answer.append('+')
  answer.append('-')

  max = max <m ? m : max
```
